About Me

I was born on July 31, 1995 in Kunming Yunnan, a very beautiful city in China. It has the most wonderful star night and fresh air. Longing for sky night and endless universe became my first crush on astronomy.

I completed my high school about science and became an undergraduate student of USTC. In Special-gifted college, I majored in physics and astrophysics. In 2017, I came to Caltech to do summer research about exoplanet. There I grew to be keen on hiking and visiting museums with wonderful paintings and sculputures.

When I am free, I love cooking, traveling and writing poems.
